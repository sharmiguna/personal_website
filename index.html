Who am I? Let me tell a little about myself -


My father named me 'Sharmila', which means 'shy and modest' in Sanskrit. Well, I am not a shy person, I tend to make friends wherever I go. I love hanging out with friends and cousins. Getting together for a coffee or a game night is something I always do. Yes, a life all alone is not what I am, I always am with someone.

But then things changed when I moved to America , away from friends and family. Though I miss being around people, I embraced having the room all for myself. I started reading books, cooking , watching movies, preparing for interviews and I never was bored or lost. So yes, I am adaptable to wherever I am and whatever happens, I deal with it. I get along with people, I believe I am a good team player.

I am an Indian, born in the ate of Tamil Nadu. My hometown is Sivakasi, it's a town known for fireworks. Though I was born there, I grew up in places around Tamil Nadu. My father moved from place to place for his job posting. That helped me make new friends when I move to new school and get adpated to the school rules and curriculum. My brother is a Software Engineer and he guided me through my college days.

I am outspoken and am a open book, I trust people. What a person likes, says alot about his personality., So things I like -
