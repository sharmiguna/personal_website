Chicken 65

It is a popular dish in the state of TamilNadu in India. Why is it named with the number 65? There are a few answers and I dont know which one is true
1) They add 65 chilli peppers for every 1 kilogram of meat
2) It needs to be marinated for atleast 65 minutes before preparation
3) The meat is required to be 65 days old to taste its best
Whatever the reason might be, it tastes the best. They marinate the chicken first, then deep fry it, then saute in onion garlic and spices with a touch of tomato sauce, chilli garlic sauce.
You have to try this spicy hot saucy chicken. Chicken 65

Bisibelebath - Sambar rice

My favorite lunch is sambar rice. The soup for the rice is cooked along with rice, Yes.. It is a one pot dish. It involves alot of vegetable cuttings. Shallots , tomatoes, curry leaves and tamarind is the basic ingredient for any indian dish. This dish needs an exclusive vegetable, it has to have atleast one of the following - eggplant, ladiesfinger, drumstick. And alongside comes the fancy vegetables like potato, cauliflower, carrot , beans and peas. Potato fry and cauliflower fry goes best with bisibelebath

Medhu vada - coconut chutney

Medhu vada is a tamil dish. We make it on special occasions, or when we have guests for breakfast. It is a tricky dish. It's not so easy to get it the right way we want it to. Vada is popular all over India. There are varieties, as Medhu vada, Dahi vada, Sambar vada, Rasa Vada.
