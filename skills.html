My undergraduate project 1 - Windows application for Dyslexia screening.


Dyslexia is a major educational problem, but the studies on genetics of dyslexia are very limited in India. Parents and teachers in INDIA are unaware of the term “dyslexia”. About 5% of the people around the world have reading disability. It is estimated that 8 to 14 per cent of the three crore schoolchildren in India are dyslexic. The English book (samacheer kalvi 2012) for the Tamil Nadu board of standard 8th has a lesson about dyslexia. Dyslexic are not mentally retarded. They use different part of their brain. Dyslexic see the BIG PICTURE. They get distracted so easily. Dyslexic are good at story telling, interior designing, 3d visual. Our project is to create an online website for dyslexia diagnosis. It has assessments for parents, teachers and students. The tests are for the children of age 5 to 12 . The tests are according to their grade level. The result gives the possibility of the child being dyslexic.
