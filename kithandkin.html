Lucky

I have always felt lucky and blessed. I was born in a beautiful country. My parents are the best in the world. A loving big brother who always tease me and fights with me, though he looks out for me. I happened to meet nice people and am part of few friends group. And my parents found the best man for me and loving fun family. I trusted them, married the guy, and I now know that my life couldn't have been any better. I feel complete and safe and happy. I met him, talked over phone, got to know him, married him in 6 months, and am living with him for over a year. I still dont get how my parents found THE GUY for me. The right one. We get along very wel.
